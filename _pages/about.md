I am a PhD student in the Department of Mechanical and Mechatronics Engineering at the University of Waterloo, advised by Professor [Amir Khajepour](https://uwaterloo.ca/mechanical-mechatronics-engineering/profile/akhajepo) and co-supervised by Professor [Ehsan Hashemi](https://apps.ualberta.ca/directory/person/ehashemi) at the University of Alberta. 
My research focuses on autonomous driving systems, with an emphasis on multi-sensor fusion, perception, motion planning, control, and cooperative perception for real-world mobility platforms. 
I led the [perception development](https://github.com/NingMingHao/Yolov8TensorrtRos) for the second generation of the [WATonoBus](https://uwaterloo.ca/watonobus/) autonomous shuttle project and led the transition to its third generation (Autoware-based platform).
I also led the development of [CoInfra](https://github.com/NingMingHao/CoInfra), a cooperative infrastructure perception system for connected and automated mobility.
My work aims to improve the reliability and safety of autonomous mobility systems in adverse weather, dense traffic, and complex indoor and outdoor environments.

<a id="research"></a>

Research
======

**Multi-sensor fusion and safe drivable space generation.** I develop perception pipelines that combine LiDAR, camera, and HD map information to estimate safe driving areas under challenging weather and road conditions.

**Motion planning and control for autonomous vehicles.** My work includes point-cloud-based potential fields and model predictive control methods that account for vehicle dynamics, passenger comfort, and perception uncertainty.

**Cooperative perception and infrastructure sensing.** I study connected sensor nodes and vehicle-to-infrastructure systems that use cameras, LiDAR, edge computing, 5G, and IoT networking to improve situational awareness for mobility platforms.

**Trustworthy autonomy and safety assessment.** I am interested in uncertainty quantification, evidence-aware safety assessment, test scenario generation, and dependable deployment of learning-enabled autonomous driving systems.

Selected project highlights
======

* **WATonoBus: Waterloo All-Weather Autonomous Shuttle.** Contributed to perception, decision making, safety monitoring, and motion planning/control for a field-tested autonomous shuttle platform.
* **CoInfra cooperative infrastructure perception.** Led work on a large-scale cooperative infrastructure perception system and dataset for vehicle-infrastructure cooperation in adverse weather.
* **5G-enabled vehicle-to-infrastructure mobility.** Developed systems using solar-powered infrastructure sensor nodes equipped with cameras, LiDAR, and Jetson edge computers for real-time data fusion, planning, and remote management.
* **Indoor cooperative perception.** Built delay-aware cooperative perception approaches for intelligent mobility in dynamic indoor environments.
