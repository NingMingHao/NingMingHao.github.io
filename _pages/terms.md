## Privacy Policy

The privacy of visitors to this academic website is important. This page summarizes the limited information that may be processed when you visit the site.

This website does not collect visitor-submitted personal information, does not host comments, and does not intentionally share personal information with third parties.

### Log Files

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may maintain standard server logs for security, reliability, and service operation. Those logs may include:

* Internet Protocol addresses (IP)
* Browser type
* Internet Service Provider (ISP)
* Date and time stamp
* Referring and exit pages
* Number of clicks

This site owner does not use those logs to identify individual visitors.

### Cookies and Web Beacons

This site does not use advertising cookies or comment-system cookies. External links, such as Google Scholar, GitHub, LinkedIn, ORCID, and arXiv, are governed by the privacy policies of those services.

#### Google Analytics

Google Analytics is not configured for this site.
